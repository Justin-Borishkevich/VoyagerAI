Frequently Asked Questions

Get Answers Fast

Is VoyagerAI a rental service or a car buying platform?

Both. You can rent vehicles short-term or begin the process to buy — our system tailors suggestions based on your goals.

Do I need an account to browse vehicles?

No account is required to view inventory, but you’ll need to sign up to reserve or schedule a test drive.

Can I schedule a test drive at my location?

Yes! VoyagerAI offers at-home test drive scheduling in supported regions. Just choose a time, and we’ll come to you.

How does AI help me pick the right vehicle?

We analyze your preferences, driving habits, and budget to suggest matches from our growing fleet of vetted vehicles.

Is my personal data secure?

Absolutely. VoyagerAI encrypts all sensitive information and follows industry best practices to protect your data.
